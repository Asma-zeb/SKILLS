# LinkedIn Product Promoter Skill

## When to Use This Skill

*   User asks to "promote a product on LinkedIn"
*   User asks for "LinkedIn post ideas for my product"
*   User needs help creating short, engaging content with hashtags for product promotion

## Procedure

1.  **Identify the product**: Understand the product's key features and benefits.
2.  **Determine the target audience**: Who are you trying to reach?
3.  **Craft a hook**: Start with a question or a bold statement related to a user problem the product solves.
4.  **Generate 2-3 short tips**: Each tip should highlight a specific benefit or use case of the product. Keep them concise and actionable.
5.  **Include a Call to Action (CTA)**: Encourage users to learn more, visit a link, or try the product.
6.  **Suggest relevant hashtags**: Include a mix of broad and specific hashtags. Aim for 3-5 hashtags.

## Output Format

**Product**: [Name of the product]
**Target Audience**: [Description of the target audience]
**Post Content**:
*   [Hook]
*   [Tip 1]
*   [Tip 2]
*   [Tip 3 (Optional)]
*   [Call to Action]
**Hashtags**:
*   #[hashtag1]
*   #[hashtag2]
*   #[hashtag3]

## Quality Criteria

*   **Conciseness**: Tips should be short and easy to digest.
*   **Benefit-oriented**: Focus on how the product helps the user.
*   **Engagement**: Hooks and CTAs should encourage interaction.
*   **Relevance**: Hashtags should be directly related to the product and its industry.
